# Feature Store

**Summary**: A feature store is a system for managing, reusing, and serving ML features consistently across training and production. The source presents it as part of the data engineering layer of MLOps. 

**Sources**: `raw/The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md`

**Last updated**: 2026-04-19

---

The article says feature stores streamline the management, reuse, and serving of features for both training and production (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md). It also states that feature stores help ensure consistency and reduce redundancy in ML workflows (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md).

## Role in MLOps

In the roadmap, feature stores appear in the data engineering and versioning phase, alongside data pipelines, ETL and ELT, validation, and metadata management (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md). This places them within the broader goal of making data inputs reliable and reproducible for production ML systems (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md).
