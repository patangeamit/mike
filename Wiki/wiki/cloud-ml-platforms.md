# Cloud ML Platforms

**Summary**: Cloud ML platforms provide managed services for training, deploying, and scaling machine learning systems. The source highlights AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud as core ecosystems for modern MLOps work. 

**Sources**: `raw/The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md`

**Last updated**: 2026-04-19

---

The source states that modern MLOps is fundamentally cloud-driven and that major cloud providers offer end-to-end platforms for training, deploying, and scaling machine learning models (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md). It specifically mentions AWS SageMaker, ECR, and EKS; Azure ML Studio and AKS; and Google Cloud Vertex AI (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md).

## Role in the Roadmap

The roadmap presents cloud ML as a late-stage capability that builds on earlier skills in containers, CI/CD, orchestration, and Kubernetes (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md). It claims that mastering cloud MLOps improves career prospects because enterprises increasingly rely on scalable, secure, and automated cloud-based ML systems (source: The Ultimate MLOps Roadmap for 2026 A Complete Guide to Becoming an MLOps Engineer.md).
